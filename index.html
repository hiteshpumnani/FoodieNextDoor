Jal Mahal Ice Cream Parlour

On Priyanshu's birthday, we enjoyed a delightful treat at Jal Mahal Ice Cream Parlour: bubble waffles with chocolate ice cream. The bubble waffle was crisp on the outside and soft inside, offering a perfect texture contrast. The rich, creamy chocolate ice cream complemented the slightly sweet and buttery flavor of the waffle. As the ice cream melted, it filled the waffle's crevices, creating a harmonious blend of flavors. Topped with chocolate syrup and colorful sprinkles, this indulgent dessert made the celebration even more special and memorable.

Pizza at Mambo's

Recently, we indulged in a delicious pizza at Mambos. The crust was perfectly crispy, topped with fresh ingredients and gooey cheese. Every bite was a delightful explosion of flavors. If you're looking for an exceptional pizza experience, Mambos is the place to go. Highly recommended!

Pasta at Tokio

We tried the pesto and arrabiatta sauce pasta at Tokio. The combination of the creamy pesto and spicy arrabiatta was simply amazing. Each bite was bursting with rich flavors and perfectly cooked pasta. If you're a pasta lover, Tokio is a must-visit for an exceptional dining experience!
